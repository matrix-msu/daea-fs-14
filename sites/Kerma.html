Geography

Kerma located in Northern Sudan, along the Nile Valley . The areas surrounding the site are rugged and very rocky. Kerma however, is a very fertile and wide plain. People were attracted to the site because of large mud-brick structures that were rising out of the ground. (1) The city is built around this mud-brick temple. Todays site is near to urban life, and is separated by a nice amount of trees from the neighboring population.
